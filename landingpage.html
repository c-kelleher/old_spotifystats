Have you ever wanted to know your top artists and songs on Spotify?

Well now you can!


Learn More

Everyone has a favorite artist or band, but sometimes it can be hard to rank one over another. Once or twice a year, Spotify will send you some selected listening stats, which are always cool to see! For many listeners, this isn't enough, and we'd like to know our most listened artists, songs, and genres all the time.

That's where we come in!

How it works:

  1. Login to Spotify by clicking the link in the top right of this page.
  2. Click "Okay" to let us access your Spotify account data, which we pull using the official Spotify Developer API.
  3. You will be redirected to a page that displays listening data, including your top artists and top songs!

All data pulled using Spotify Web API.
